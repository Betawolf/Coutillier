This file is a checklist of Gendarme 2.10 rules for Coutillier to implement.
Those rules with an [a] next to them have been implemented and are part of what
Coutillier checks. Those with a [x] next to them have been examined and found
to be unimplementable in Coutillier, or have been dropped for other reasons.
Those rules with an empty box [ ] have yet to be worked on.
